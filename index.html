About Me

Greetings! My name is Amy MacKinnon and I’m a junior developer proficient in Ruby, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, jQuery, and HTML/CSS.

I have been building static websites since 1999 but recently went through a 3-month long intensive full-time bootcamp to increase my skills in back-end web development. I’m fulfilling a life long dream and enjoying myself every step of the way. If you're interested in this journey, you can read about it on my blog.

Other interests and hobbies include: baking pies, live theatre, taking care of my cats, a Star Trek Club, and many other interests that keep my time quite filled. I sometimes think it’s a curse to be so interested in what there is out there in the world. This website highlights only a small part of my adventures.
