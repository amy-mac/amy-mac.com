About Me

Greetings! My name is Amy MacKinnon and I’m a web developer, thespian, and mother.

I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, but have also lived in Idaho, Colorado, and Virginia before returning home. I’ve worked in a few different fields and as Xena would say, “I have many skills.” My greatest passion in life is theatre, followed by computers & technology. I have two young daughters who take up most of my free time but I still make time to pursue my interests.

Those interests and hobbies include: baking pies, cosplaying, gardening, cuddling with my cats, reading books and comics, crocheting/knitting, playing board games and video games, performing theatre, seeing theatre, playing piano, tap dancing, and more. I'm extremely fascinated by just about everything out there in the world. This website highlights only a small part of my adventures.
